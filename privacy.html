This Privacy Policy applies to Easy Slip (Beijing) Technology Co., Ltd.(hereinafter, "we") mobile application [24 Solar Terms] (hereinafter, "Our App") and sets out how we collect, use, protect and share the personal information of our app users.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following information:

Information that you provide in our app, such as your name, email address, and password.

Information that we automatically collect when you use our app, such as your device model, operating system version, device identifiers, location information, and information about your activities in our app.

We may use your information to:

Provide and improve the functionalities of our app.

Respond to your inquiries and requests.

Send you updates and marketing information about our app.

3. How We Share Your Information

We will not share your personal information with third parties unless otherwise stated in this Privacy Policy.

4. Permissions Requested Within the App

We need to request certain system permissions related to personal privacy to ensure that some features within the app can function properly. We will request your authorization when you use these features, and we will only access your personal information after obtaining your explicit consent. If you do not need to use these features, you can refuse authorization; if you have never used these features, we will not request permissions or access your personal information.

The permissions we will request and their corresponding features include:

Permissions Requested in the Android App

* Allow writing to external storage: When you save generated images to your phone, we will request this permission. After authorization, you can download the generated images to your phone's local storage; when you perform mobile number verification, after authorization, it will be used to write SD card information to complete the number verification.

* Allow reading from external storage: When you upload images while creating or editing content, we will request this permission.

* Allow camera access or reading from the photo album: When you take photos or add images while creating or editing content, we will request this permission.

* Allow notifications: We will notify you when a solar term arrives.

* Allow vibration: When you use the app for the first time, we will request this permission. After authorization, you will receive better vibration feedback during input, enhancing the typing experience.

* Allow reading the device ID: This is used for product feature analysis and improvement without associating it with your identity.

Permissions Requested in the iOS App

* Allow access to the system photo album: When you need to upload images from the photo album while creating or editing content, we will request this permission.

* Allow writing to the system photo album: When you save images from shared content to your phone's local storage, we will request this permission.

* Allow notifications: We will notify you when a solar term arrives.

* Allow reading the device ID: This is used for product feature analysis and improvement without associating it with your identity.

5. Third-Party SDK Usage Instructions

In order to provide you with push notification services, our application integrates the Alibaba Cloud EMAS Mobile Push SDK. This SDK is provided by Alibaba Cloud Computing Co., Ltd. and is used to enable message reception and push functionality. During usage, the Alibaba Cloud Push SDK may collect the following information:

Device information: Includes device identifiers (such as IMEI, Android ID, OAID), device model, operating system version, etc., used to identify the device and ensure accurate message delivery.

Network information: Includes IP address, network type (such as WiFi, 4G/5G), etc., used to optimize the stability and speed of message pushing.

App information: Includes app package name, version number, etc., used to ensure compatibility between the push service and the app.

Push log information: Includes logs of message sending, receiving, and clicks, used for statistics on push effectiveness and analysis of user behavior.

The Alibaba Cloud Push SDK strictly complies with relevant laws and regulations and takes reasonable security measures to protect your personal information. You can learn more about its data processing and protection practices through Alibaba Cloud's privacy policy: [ https://terms.aliyun.com/legal-agreement/terms/suit_bu1_ali_cloud/suit_bu1_ali_cloud202112071754_83380.html?spm=a2c4g.11186623.0.0.13cb6493lR6GH2 ].

We use the Alibaba Cloud Push SDK solely for the purpose of implementing the push service and will not use it for other purposes. You can disable push notifications through your device's system settings, but this may affect the normal use of some features.
